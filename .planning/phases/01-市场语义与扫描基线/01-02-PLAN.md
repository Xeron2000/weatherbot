## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| External market/weather payloads → scan loop | 上游字段缺失、单位异常、规则文本变化可能污染本地 universe |
| Scan loop → persisted market JSON | 错误字段写入会成为后续 phase 的错误真相来源 |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-1-11 | T | `scan_and_update()` | mitigate | guardrail 失败时写 `skipped` + `skip_reasons`，禁止继续进入候选/持仓逻辑 |
| T-1-12 | I | `new_market()` / persisted JSON | mitigate | 在 market record 中持久化 station/unit/rule text/condition-token 标识，避免后续 phase 重新猜测 |
| T-1-13 | D | full scan loop | mitigate | 单个 market 校验失败后 `save_market(mkt)` 并 `continue`，不得抛异常终止整轮 scan |
</threat_model>
